unichance.xyz is a free and anonymous tool for looking at university acceptance rates, especially for international students.

Some universities are notorious for having highly variable acceptance rates for international students. unichance.xyz provides powerful tools for drilling down, helping students find the universities they want to apply for with high priority, as well as reason about which universities they can still expect a response from.
